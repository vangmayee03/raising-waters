Introduction

Flood forecasting is the use of forecast precipitation and streamflow data in rainfall-runoff and streamflow routing models to forecast flow rates and water levels. Flood forecasting can also make use of real-time rainfall and river level data to create models that detect flood warning.

Effective real-time flood forecasting models could be useful for early warning and disaster prevention. This system applies machine learning techniques to predict potential flood occurrences.
